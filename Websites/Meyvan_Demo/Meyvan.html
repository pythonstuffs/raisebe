About Us

Experience Quality Products & Exceptional Service with Us!

Meyvan international, established in the year 2018 by management graduates, is engaged into catering various consumable needs of customers into sectors like engineering, automotive, power, automation, robotics, Woods and plastic etc.We are manufacturer and authorised channel partner for renowned multinationals for various industrial consumable products. We implement standard ethical practices and provide best possible solutions to every critical need of end customers. In new era of industrialisation, many customers find it difficult to get exact, reliable and cost- beneficial solutions for their consumable needs due to large number of material suppliers available in the market & which provide different solutions for the same application, incurring more time, money & product confusion just for finding single source for one application.We, at Meyvan International, having tie ups with number of material manufacturers, understand your consumable needs to determine the best fit for your application. We work closely & continuously to have an optimum, reliable & cost beneficial solutions for your system.Meyvan international, having number of multinational manufacturers under one roof, also provides free consultation and audit it order to save your time & efforts to fulfil every critical need of your applications.
